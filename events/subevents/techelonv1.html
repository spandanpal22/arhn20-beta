Techmela
TECH MELA, the annual Science Exhibition of NIT Durgapur is a unique way for students to pose questions for which they seek out answers and to satisfy their own curiosity about the world around them. TECH MELA is an experiment, a demonstration, a research effort, a collection of scientific items and display of scientific apparatus presented for viewing.TECH MELA is the ultimate answer to the often-asked student question: "Why do I need to learn this stuff, anyway?" It integrates, into one functional activity, virtually all of the skills and arts that are usually taught separately (sometimes not at all or without obvious "purpose") in many schools. When brought to completion, the project is an amalgamation of reading, writing, spelling, grammar, math, statistics, ethics, logic, critical thinking, computer science, graphic arts, scientific methodology, self- learning of one or more technical or specialty fields, and (if the project qualifies for formal competition) public speaking and defense in front of expert judges.So, Guys and girls, gear up as we head for the TECHMELA, the annual Science exhibition to be held during AAROHAN 2018. We invite entries for the exhibition. Mail us your ideas, innovations and projects on any topic and you will have them displayed during the fest. The entries should be feasible and working. Winners will be awarded UNESCO acclaimed IEEE certificates, cash prize and vouchers.
