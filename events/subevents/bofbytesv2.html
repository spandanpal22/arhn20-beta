Khula Maidan
The event aims at encouraging the creative inclination of students as well as providing a technical platform for designers and design aspirants.

Judging criteria:

Round 1: Designing based aptitude questions which will test the reasoning and creative ability of the participant will constitute the common qualifying section.

Round 2: The participants will be expected to solve a set of design related problems and come up with creative and economical designs to suit the requirements.

Round 3: The finalists will have to present their idea into any interface (if app based) or present the creative depiction of the oeuvre and its relevancy to the problem statement through a presentation.
