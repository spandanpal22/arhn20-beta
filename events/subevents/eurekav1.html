Colossus
Colossus is a fun event where you just play by your instincts and smartness. Nothing related to marketing or sales whatsoever. No intricate calculations required!

The main round is similar to Bluffmaster, where the participants have to guess the probable answers to a given question. Higher the answer ranks on our pre-defined list of the possible answers, more are the points awarded. Each question round has a secret Bluffmaster team which has all the answers to that question. At the end of each round, the Bluffmaster team is tried to be identified by other teams.

"So have you got it in you, the kind of savvy to steal the show? Or will you tumble on your own bluff?"

B-plan
“Having a plan will take you a step further”

All those budding entrepreneurial minds that had a spirit to turn their imaginations into reality, started it all with the blueprint of their success story, i.e. a Business Plan. A Business Plan, a necessary tool for all businesses is a formal statement of business goals, reasons they are attainable, and plans for reaching them. A Business Plan must include Startup Analysis, Competitive Analysis, Customer Analysis, Strategy Implementation, Marketing Plan, and Financial Plan. A well written B-PLAN helps the team to do SWOT (Strength, Weakness, Opportunity, Threats) Analysis.

The Participants have to register at https://tinyurl.com/arhn-bplan2018 for the competition with a team consists of maximum 4 numbers of members including. They are expected to submit a well structured B-Plan written according to a specified format within a deadline.

The judging for the best B-Plan will be done on the basis of financial viability, organizational capacity building, communication and marketing skills etc.

Selldom
"Everyone lives by selling something".

Poster idea for Selldom: It is a real life sales event in which every team is expected to come up with an idea to earn real money by providing a service. The teams will be given a certain amount of time and an initial amount of money. They can spend the money in anything to come up with an selling idea to make more profit out of it. The team with the maximum profit wins.
