Abhivyakti
If you have been articulating an idea and are looking for a platform to express your viewpoint, we give you a chance to 'ABHIVYAKT ' yourself. Just Pen down your original unconventional ideas and we will bring recognition to you. 'ABHIVYAKTI' is the annual paper presentation contest of AAROHAN, wherein students put forward their technical innovations and present their ideas in any field of science and technology in a genuine and comprehensive way. It is not just a competition, but the main idea behind this is to put people forward with their ideas, through these, audiences are exposed to speakers who are doing cutting edge work across numerous causes. The speakers often demonstrate constructive ways that people can help with these causes on a personal level. It is great arena for presenters, speakers to bring down your ideas to life. It is a one day event and all winning entries are IEEE certified articulating an idea and.
For the time being presenters should have:
  1. Hard copy of the material that he/she may be presenting.
  2. A power point presentation (PPT)
  3. (Optional) prototypes for demonstration etc.

Today's science fiction is tomorrow's science fact.
- *Isaac Asimov*.
Are you fascinated with sci-fi movies? Is your mind full of fan theories? Do you sometimes get lost in the world of imagination, which are governed by your own predicted laws? Then you are in the right place to give your imagination colorful wings. This AAROHAN 2018, we bring to you CONJECTURE- unique event where you could unleash your imagination, comprehend the unseen, conceptualize your ideas and prove your uniqueness, who knows you may strike the winning position. This AAROHAN, unleash the perceptive in you.
We will be having three rounds
  1. Paper pen quiz.
  2. Crack the Enigma.
  3. Final Master Code.
These rounds will unfold itself as you and your team progresses and there is number of ways to win here. Your decision will decide your fate, that is CONJECTURE.
